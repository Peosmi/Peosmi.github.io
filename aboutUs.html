About Us:

We are an online women's designer fashion rental hub, dedicated to revolutionising the way you access and experience sustainable fashion. Our mission is to make ethical and sustainable renting over buying easier and more accessible, providing you with a wide range of clothing rentals sourced exclusively from ethical clothing brands.

In creating our platform, we have partnered with reputable designer fashion rental businesses in New Zealand, ensuring that every rental option available aligns with our commitment to ethical practices. By collaborating with these businesses, we are able to offer you the largest range and widest size range of clothing rentals, catering to various styles and preferences.

As part of our commitment to innovation, we are proud to introduce the option of virtual try-ons, allowing you to digitally experience our clothing rentals from the comfort of your own home. With our virtual fitting room, you can confidently choose the perfect outfit for your next event, ensuring the best fit and style.

Join us in embracing the future of sustainable fashion. Together, we can make a positive impact on the environment and support ethical practices within the fashion industry. Browse our extensive collection of clothing rentals, experience the convenience of virtual try-ons, and make a conscious choice to rent instead of buy.
